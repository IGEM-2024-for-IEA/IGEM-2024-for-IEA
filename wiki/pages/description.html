PFAS, or "forever chemicals," are ubiquitous and cause cancers, infertility, birth defects, vaccine resistance, and other serious health problems at very low concentrations. Current methods to degrade PFAS are costly and could result in a highly toxic byproduct. Our high school iGEM team is using AI to design enzymes that will destroy PFAS chemicals. Once we have perfected the design, we will test our novel enzymes experimentally, using a cutting-edge high-throughput testing method. We are working at a lab at Caltech and are trying to raise funds to allow our team to pay the iGEM fees and to travel to Paris from Pasadena, California.
